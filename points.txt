High-Impact (7 Points Each): These are major items that require significant investment and have a huge environmental benefit (e.g., On-site renewable energy, Energy-efficient HVAC, Wastewater treatment, Diverting construction waste).

Medium-Impact (6 Points Each): These are also critical items, often mandatory for higher ratings (e.g., Rainwater harvesting, Maximized daylighting).

Standard (3 Points Each): These are all the other 20 items, which represent foundational green building practices.

(4 items * 7 pts) + (2 items * 6 pts) + (20 items * 3 pts) = 100 Total Points.

The server will now add up the points for every box you check. The rating will be based on this aggregate score:

Platinum: 75-100 points

Gold: 60-74 points

Silver: 50-59 points

Certified: 40-49 points

Not Certified: < 40 points
